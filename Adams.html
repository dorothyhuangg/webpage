About Us:

We at A&L Landscape and Design take pride in our work. What started as a one person company, Bernabe "Adrian" Lopez has turned this company into a growing, successful corporation that has left an impact on every house we have worked with. Since 2014, we have been providing our services to the nearby coomunities of North Andover, Andover, Lynnfield, North Reading, and neighboring towns. Our services include Irrigation, Mowing, Weedng, Trimming, Fall/Spring Cleanups, as well as Hardscape. We are licensed and insured to take on any request and turn every client's dreams into reality.

Service

Irrigation Services: Whether it is shutting down your irrgiation system before the cold or you need to install a whole new irrigation system, we are here for you. It can even be fixing one singluar irrigation head, give us a call. We are highly experienced in this area.

Mowing and Weeding: What started as our one of our two services we offered has now expanded into a specialzied craft of ours. Our guys treat your grass with much care and make sure to cut your grass on schedule. Thanks to our vast cutting machines, no lawn is out of reach for us.

Hardscape Construction: We do it all. Patios, stairs, walls, firepits, driveways, as well as any outside repairs.

Fall/Spring CleanUp: No matter the season, we will make sure your house is well taken care of. Once the fall comes around, we will make sure to clear up leaves and branches from your property. We will also prepare and clean your yard and graden for the spring time. Spring cleanups can include mulching, trimming, weeding, and anything of your desire. We want to make sure your property is looking fresh and clean all year round

Snow Plowing: For the last couple of years, we started to provide snow cleanup. Whether you want one round of plowing or 5, we will make sure to have your driveways, walkways, and stairs clear of snow.

And Much More! We also do tree cutting, fencing, and plant/tree planting.
